How it works

Coaching is hard enough.
Planning shouldn't be.

Set up once, and Sportified works with you every session after that — adapting to what's actually happening on the ground.

01

Set Your Session

Enter your sport, group, and goals once. From that point forward, Sportified already knows your setup and builds every future session around what you've established.

02

Get Your Plan

Build your session your way — create it manually or let Sportified generate one for you. Either way, you're ready to go in under a minute.

03

Adapt On the Fly

Weather change? Half your players absent? One tap rebuilds the whole session around your new reality — no scrambling, no stress.

04

Track What Happened

After each session, log what worked and what didn't. That feedback shapes your next plan — so the more you use it, the better it gets.
