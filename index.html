How it works

Coaching is hard enough.
Planning shouldn't be.

Tell Sportified your sport, age group, and time. Get a complete, structured session — and adapt it instantly when anything changes.

01

Set your session

Enter your sport, group size, age, available time, and any equipment on hand. Takes under 30 seconds.

02

Get your plan

A full session appears instantly — warm-up, main block, cool-down — structured, timed, and ready to run.

03

Adapt on the fly

Weather change? Half your players absent? One tap rebuilds the whole session around your new reality.

04

Track what happened

After each session, your program gets automatic summaries — what was covered, intensity, and athlete progress.
